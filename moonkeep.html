MoonKeep · iOS & Android

Every session, accounted for.

Prepaid packages are easy to buy and hard to keep track of. Ten facials here, a massage credit there, store value at a clinic across town — and an expiry date quietly counting down on each one. MoonKeep keeps the ledger so you don't have to.

Track any kind of package

Session bundles, credit pools, prepaid store value, monthly memberships — including bonus and free sessions, and bundled treatments drawn from a shared pool. MoonKeep models how these packages actually work, not just a counter.

Know if it was worth it

Pacing tells you whether you're on track to use everything before it expires. Break-even shows the moment a package has paid for itself against walk-in prices. Insights charts your visit rhythm across clinics and treatments.

Effortless logging

  • Log a session in a couple of taps, with notes and dates
  • Scan a receipt or package brochure — OCR fills in the details
  • Schedule appointments with reminders, per treatment
  • Expiry banners before anything slips away

Private by default

Everything lives on your device. No account, no cloud, no tracking of your treatments. Read the full privacy policy.
