About

Small studio.
Deliberate software.

SSCIM is a small independent studio. We make apps for the parts of life that don't need a platform, a feed, or an account — just a well-made tool that's pleasant to open and easy to trust.

That means editorial design over dashboards, on-device data over clouds, and features that earn their place. If an app can't be explained in a sentence, we keep sanding it down until it can.

i.

One job, done well

Each app has a single clear purpose. Depth comes from craft, not from feature count.

ii.

Your data stays yours

We default to on-device storage. No accounts, no sync you didn't ask for, nothing to leak.

iii.

Calm by design

Quiet palettes, generous spacing, no badges begging for attention. Software as good company.
