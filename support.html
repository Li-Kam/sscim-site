Common questions

Is my data backed up anywhere?
MoonKeep stores everything on your device only. Back up your phone (iCloud or Android backup) to keep your data safe, and use the CSV export in the app to keep your own copy of your history.

Can I move my data to a new phone?
Restoring your new phone from a device backup carries MoonKeep's data across with it.

I found a bug.
Email us with the app version (shown in Settings), your device model, and what you were doing when it happened — screenshots help a lot.
