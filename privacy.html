Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026 · Applies to MoonKeep for iOS and Android.

SSCIM builds apps that are private by default. This policy explains what data MoonKeep handles, where it lives, and what — if anything — leaves your device.

Data you put into MoonKeep

Packages, treatments, session logs, notes, appointments, prices, and clinic details you enter are stored in a local database on your device. We do not operate accounts or servers for this data: it is never uploaded to us, and we cannot see it. Deleting the app deletes this data.

Receipt scanning (OCR)

When you scan a receipt or brochure, the image is processed to extract text and the recognised details are stored in the same local database. Images and recognised text are not sent to SSCIM.

Notifications

Expiry and appointment reminders are scheduled locally on your device. They do not involve a server.

Advertising

MoonKeep may show ads served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage signals to serve and measure ads, as described in Google's privacy policy. Ad content never has access to the packages or sessions you record in the app.

What we don't do

  • No accounts, sign-ins, or profiles
  • No selling of personal data
  • No access to your health or treatment records — your entries stay on your device

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date.
